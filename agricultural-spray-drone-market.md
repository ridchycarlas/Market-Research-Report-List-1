<p><p>The agricultural spray drone market comprises three primary types: battery-powered, hybrid drive, and petrol-powered drones. </p><p>Battery-powered drones are favored for their eco-friendliness and lower operating costs, relying solely on electric energy for efficient crop monitoring and spraying. Hybrid drive drones combine electric and petrol power, offering flexibility and extended flight time, making them suitable for larger farms. Petrol-powered drones are typically more powerful and have longer operational ranges, making them ideal for extensive agricultural areas but may have higher environmental impacts.</p></p>
