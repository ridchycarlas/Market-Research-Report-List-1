<p><p>Color Filter Arrays (CFAs) are essential in digital imaging, enabling sensors to capture color information. The Bayer Filter, the most widely used, consists of a grid of red, green, and blue filters. The RGBE Filter incorporates an additional emerald filter for enhanced color accuracy. The RYYB Filter replaces the green filter with yellow, improving low-light performance and enhancing brightness. Other market types include specialized CFAs for specific applications, such as infrared sensing or multispectral imaging, catering to diverse imaging needs.</p></p>
