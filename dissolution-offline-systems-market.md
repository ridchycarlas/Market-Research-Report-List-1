<p><strong>The Dissolution Offline Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Offline Systems</li><li>VIS Offline Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolution Offline Systems encompass testing methods that evaluate the release of active pharmaceutical ingredients from dosage forms in a controlled environment. The UV Offline Systems utilize ultraviolet light to measure absorbance of dissolving drugs, providing precise quantification of dissolution profiles. In contrast, VIS Offline Systems utilize visible light for similar measurements, often suited for formulations with chromophoric properties. Both systems play crucial roles in pharmaceutical quality control, ensuring that products meet regulatory standards for efficacy and safety.</p></p>
