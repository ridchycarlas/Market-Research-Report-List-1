<p><p>The Automatic Sample Stainer market comprises two primary types: Continuous Feed Stainers and Batch Stainers. Continuous Feed Stainers are designed for high-throughput environments, allowing for uninterrupted operation and a steady flow of samples, making them ideal for large laboratories. In contrast, Batch Stainers process samples in discrete batches, offering flexibility and control over staining protocols, suitable for varied sample types. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce manual handling, and improve staining accuracy in histopathology and cytology applications.</p></p>
