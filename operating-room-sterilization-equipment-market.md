<p><ul><li>High Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Operating Room Sterilization Equipment market is categorized into high-temperature and low-temperature sterilization methods. High-temperature sterilization typically involves steam or dry heat processes, effectively eliminating microorganisms and ensuring instrument safety. It is commonly used for heat-resistant materials. In contrast, low-temperature sterilization employs methods like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide gas plasma, suitable for heat-sensitive instruments. This differentiation allows healthcare facilities to select appropriate sterilization techniques based on the type of instruments and materials used, ensuring optimal safety and efficacy.</p></p>
