<p><p>Hyperspectral Imaging (HSI) captures images across various wavelengths beyond the visible spectrum, enabling detailed material analysis. The market is categorized into several types: Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) focuses on wavelengths from 400 to 1000 nm, ideal for agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) covers 1000 to 2500 nm, useful for mineralogy and vegetation analysis. Medium-Wave Infrared (MWIR) and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) are employed in thermal imaging and surveillance applications. Other markets encompass specialized sectors like defense and food quality control.</p></p>
