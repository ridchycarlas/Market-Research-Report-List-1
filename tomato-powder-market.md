<p><p>The tomato powder market comprises various types, including spray dried, freeze dried, and others. Spray dried tomato powder is produced by rapidly drying tomato paste using hot air, preserving flavor and color. Freeze dried tomato powder involves freezing tomatoes and then removing moisture through sublimation, resulting in a vibrant color and enhanced nutrients. Other methods may include sun drying or vacuum drying. Each type offers distinct qualities, catering to different culinary applications and consumer preferences in the food industry.</p></p>
