<p><p>The industrial valve market includes various types designed for specific applications. Gate valves allow or block flow with a wedge mechanism, ideal for on/off control. Globe valves regulate flow through a movable disk and seat, providing precise control. Ball valves offer quick shutoff with a spherical closure element. Plug valves use a cylindrical or tapered plug for flow control. Buffer valves manage pressure or flow relief, while other types include check valves and butterfly valves, serving diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
