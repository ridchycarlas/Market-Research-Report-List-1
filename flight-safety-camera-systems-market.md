<p><p>The Flight Safety Camera Systems market is categorized into two primary types: In-Cabin and Out-Cabin systems. In-Cabin camera systems are installed within the aircraft's interior, focusing on passenger safety, crew interactions, and monitoring cabin conditions to enhance security and emergency response. Out-Cabin camera systems are placed externally, capturing footage of the aircraft's exterior, runway conditions, and surrounding environments to assist in ground operations, surveillance, and assessing potential hazards during takeoff and landing. Both types are crucial for overall flight safety.</p></p>
