<p><p>The Cloud Fax Services market is primarily divided into two types: Subscription-Based Service and Pay-Per-Use Fax Service. Subscription-Based Services offer users a fixed monthly fee for unlimited faxing, making it ideal for businesses with consistent faxing needs. In contrast, Pay-Per-Use Fax Services charge users based on the volume of faxes sent, providing flexibility for those with sporadic requirements or smaller businesses. Both models provide cost-effective, efficient ways to send and receive faxes without the need for physical infrastructure.</p></p>
