<p><strong>The Hydronic Floor Heating Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Above-floor Systems</li><li>Below-floor Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydronic floor heating systems are categorized into two main types: above-floor and below-floor systems. Above-floor systems involve the installation of heating pipes or mats over the existing floor, making them easier to retro-fit in existing buildings without major renovations. Below-floor systems, on the other hand, incorporate heating elements within the concrete slab or underfloor structure during construction, providing efficient and uniform heat distribution. Both types offer effective radiant heating solutions, enhancing comfort while optimizing energy efficiency in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
