<p><p>Silicon nitride ceramics are classified into several market types based on their composition and manufacturing processes. The CPS (Conventional Processed Silicon Nitride) features standard processing techniques, offering good mechanical properties. RS (Reinforced Silicon Nitride) incorporates additional materials for enhanced strength and toughness. GPS (Gas Pressure Sintered) involves a high-pressure sintering technique, resulting in superior density and performance. Other types may include variations or hybrid forms that combine different properties for specialized applications, catering to industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
