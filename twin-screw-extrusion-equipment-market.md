<p><strong>The Twin Screw Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meshing Type Extrusion Equipment</li><li>Non Meshing Type Extrusion Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Twin Screw Extrusion Equipment market is categorized into two main types: meshing and non-meshing types. Meshing type extrusion equipment features interlocking screws that enhance material mixing and processing efficiency, making it ideal for applications requiring thorough blending. In contrast, non-meshing type equipment has parallel screws that allow for more straightforward material flow, suitable for less complex processing tasks. Both types cater to varied industries, including food, plastics, and pharmaceuticals, each offering distinct advantages based on processing requirements.</p></p>
