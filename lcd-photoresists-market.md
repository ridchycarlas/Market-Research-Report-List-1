<p><strong>The LCD Photoresists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Color Resists(RGB)</li><li>Black Matrix(BM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD photoresists market can be categorized into Color Resists (RGB) and Black Matrix (BM) types. Color Resists are essential for creating vibrant displays by transferring RGB (Red, Green, Blue) patterns onto the LCD panel, enhancing color accuracy and brightness. In contrast, Black Matrix resists are used to block light between pixels, improving contrast and reducing color cross-talk. Both types play a crucial role in the production of high-quality LCD screens, catering to the demand for superior visual experiences in consumer electronics.</p></p>
