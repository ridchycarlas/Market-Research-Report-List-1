<p><strong>The Nuclear Power Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Globe Valve</li><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power valve market consists of various types of valves essential for safety and efficiency. Globe valves regulate flow with a spherical body design, while ball valves provide quick shut-off capabilities with a rotating ball mechanism. Butterfly valves are suited for large volume control, utilizing a disk to manage flow. Gate valves are ideal for on/off service, allowing full flow when open. Diaphragm valves ensure leak-tight sealing and are used in critical applications, making them vital in nuclear power systems.</p></p>
