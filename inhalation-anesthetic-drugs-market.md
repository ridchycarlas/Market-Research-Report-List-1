<p><p>The inhalation anesthetic drugs market is primarily divided into two segments: general anesthesia drugs and local anesthesia drugs. General anesthesia drugs induce a state of unconsciousness and are used for major surgical procedures, ensuring the patient feels no pain during surgery. In contrast, local anesthesia drugs block sensation in a specific area of the body, allowing minor surgical or dental procedures to be performed while the patient remains awake and aware. Both segments are essential for patient management in various medical settings.</p></p>
