<p><p>The companion diagnostic market can be categorized primarily into immunohistochemistry and molecular diagnostics. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) involves using antibodies to detect specific proteins in tissue samples, helping in cancer treatment decisions by identifying target expressions. Molecular diagnostics, on the other hand, focuses on analyzing genetic material to identify mutations or pathogen DNA/RNA, which aids in tailoring precise therapies. Both types enhance personalized medicine, ensuring that patients receive the most effective treatments based on their unique diagnostic profiles.</p></p>
