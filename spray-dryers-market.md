<p><ul><li>Pressure Spray Dryer</li><li>Stream Spray Dryer</li><li>Centrifuging Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray dryer market comprises three main types: Pressure Spray Dryers, Stream Spray Dryers, and Centrifuging Spray Dryers. Pressure Spray Dryers use high pressure to atomize liquids into droplets, enabling rapid drying. Stream Spray Dryers utilize air streams to disperse the liquid, offering efficient drying for heat-sensitive materials. Centrifuging Spray Dryers employ a centrifugal force to atomize liquids, providing uniform droplet sizes and high drying efficiency. Each type caters to specific applications across various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
