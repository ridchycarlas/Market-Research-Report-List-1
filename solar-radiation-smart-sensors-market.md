<p><p>The Solar Radiation Smart Sensors market features two primary types: Silicon Cell Type and Thermopile Type. Silicon Cell Type sensors utilize photovoltaic cells to measure solar irradiance, offering advantages in cost-effectiveness and efficiency. In contrast, Thermopile Type sensors operate based on temperature differentials, effectively capturing infrared radiation, making them suitable for diverse environmental conditions. Both types enhance solar energy management and optimize efficiency in applications ranging from weather monitoring to photovoltaic system performance assessment, driving advancements in renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
