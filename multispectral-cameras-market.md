<p><p>The multispectral camera market is primarily divided into two types: single-lens multispectral cameras and multi-lens multispectral cameras. Single-lens multispectral cameras utilize one lens to capture multiple spectral bands, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for many applications. In contrast, multi-lens multispectral cameras use separate lenses for different spectral bands, providing higher resolution and accuracy, making them suitable for detailed analysis in sectors like agriculture, environmental monitoring, and remote sensing. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
