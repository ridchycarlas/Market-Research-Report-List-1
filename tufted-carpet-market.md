<p><strong>The Tufted Carpet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cut Pile</li><li>Loop Pile</li><li>Cut and Loop Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tufted carpet market encompasses three primary types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile features fibers that are sheared, resulting in a soft, plush surface ideal for comfort and luxury. Loop pile consists of uncut loops, offering durability and a classic look, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Cut and loop pile combines both techniques, creating textured patterns and varying heights, providing visual interest and versatility. Each type serves distinct aesthetic and functional needs for consumers.</p></p>
