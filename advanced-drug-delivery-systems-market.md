<p><p>The Advanced Drug Delivery Systems market encompasses various methods tailored for efficient drug administration. Oral drug delivery involves ingesting formulations for systemic absorption via the digestive tract. Pulmonary drug delivery utilizes inhalation techniques for direct lung targeting, enhancing bioavailability. Transdermal drug delivery employs skin patches to facilitate sustained drug release into the bloodstream. Parenteral drug delivery encompasses injections or infusions, allowing immediate effects and controlled dosages. Each method aims to optimize therapeutic efficacy and patient compliance.</p></p>
