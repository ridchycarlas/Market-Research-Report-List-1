<p><strong>The Heated Separators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heated separators are classified into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operational efficiency. Vertical heated separators utilize gravity to enhance separation efficiency, making them suitable for applications with low flow rates and higher viscosity fluids. In contrast, horizontal heated separators provide a larger surface area for phase separation, ideal for high flow rates and varying fluid compositions. Both types are crucial in oil and gas processing, ensuring effective separation of liquids and gases under controlled temperatures.</p></p>
