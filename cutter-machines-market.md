<p><p>Cutter machines are categorized into several types based on their drive mechanisms and functionalities. Mechanical transmission cutting machines utilize gears and levers for operation, offering reliability and efficiency for various materials. Hydraulic drive cutting machines use hydraulic pressure for powerful cuts, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Automatic rolling type cutting machines streamline production through automated processes. Computer-controlled water beam cutting machines employ high-pressure water jets for precision cutting in intricate designs. Other emerging technologies may include laser and plasma cutting machines, expanding market applications.</p></p>
