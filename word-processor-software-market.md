<p><p>The word processor software market is divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. In contrast, cloud-based word processors are hosted online, allowing users to access and collaborate in real-time from any device with internet connectivity. Both types cater to different user needs, with on-premise appealing to those valuing security and control, while cloud solutions emphasize flexibility and collaboration.</p></p>
