<p><p>The industrial turntable market is categorized into powered and manual types. Powered industrial turntables utilize electric motors to facilitate rotation and repositioning of heavy loads, enhancing efficiency and reducing operator strain. They are ideal for automated processes in manufacturing and warehousing. Conversely, manual industrial turntables rely on human effort for rotation, offering a cost-effective solution for small-scale operations or environments where power sources are limited. Both types serve essential roles in improving material handling and workflow in various industries.</p></p>
