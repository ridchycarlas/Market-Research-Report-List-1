<p><strong>The Military Specification Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Specification Resistor market is categorized into two main types: Through Hole and Surface Mount. Through Hole resistors feature leads that penetrate the circuit board, providing robust mechanical support, making them suitable for high-reliability applications. In contrast, Surface Mount resistors are designed to sit flat on the PCB, allowing for higher density and compact assembly, which is ideal for modern military electronics. Both types meet stringent military standards for performance and durability in demanding environments.</p></p>
