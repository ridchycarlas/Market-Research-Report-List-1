<p><p>The Transport Management System (TMS) market encompasses various types that enhance supply chain efficiency. Transport Sourcing facilitates vendor selection and rate negotiation. Capacity Management optimizes resource utilization to meet demand. Performance Management tracks and analyzes transportation metrics for continuous improvement. Network Design focuses on establishing optimal logistics networks for cost-effectiveness. Shipping Consolidation combines shipments to minimize costs and enhance efficiency. Route Planning determines the most efficient delivery paths, reducing transit time and expenses while improving service levels.</p></p>
