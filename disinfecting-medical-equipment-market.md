<p><p>The disinfecting medical equipment market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Steam Disinfecting** utilizes high-pressure steam to eliminate pathogens effectively, suitable for heat-resistant instruments.</p><p>2. **Dry Heat Disinfecting** employs elevated temperatures without moisture, ideal for materials sensitive to steam or chemicals.</p><p>3. **Chemical Disinfecting** involves the use of liquid or gas disinfectants to kill microorganisms, often used for heat-sensitive equipment. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to various medical instruments and operational requirements in healthcare settings.</p></p>
