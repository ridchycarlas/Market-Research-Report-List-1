<p><p>The textile coatings market is primarily categorized into thermoplastics, thermosets, and other types. Thermoset coatings, on the other hand, cure permanently, offering enhanced heat, chemical, and wear resistance, ideal for demanding conditions. Other market types may include bio-based and hybrid coatings, which cater to eco-friendly trends and unique performance requirements. These diverse options enable tailored solutions for industries such as automotive, aerospace, and fashion.</p></p>
