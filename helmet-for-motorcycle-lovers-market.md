<p><p>The motorcycle helmet market comprises three main types: Full Face, Open Face, and Half Helmets. Full Face Helmets provide maximum protection by covering the entire head with a solid chin bar, making them ideal for high-speed riding. Open Face Helmets offer a balance of protection and visibility, covering the top, back, and sides of the head while leaving the face exposed, appealing to casual riders. Half Helmets, or brain buckets, provide minimal coverage, prioritizing comfort and style over protection, often favored for short trips or cruising.</p></p>
