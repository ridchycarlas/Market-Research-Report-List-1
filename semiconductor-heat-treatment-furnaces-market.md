<p><strong>The Semiconductor Heat Treatment Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Furnace</li><li>Vertical Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor heat treatment furnaces market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical furnaces. Horizontal furnaces feature a design that allows wafers to be processed in a flat position, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and efficient heat transfer. They are suitable for batch processing. In contrast, vertical furnaces operate with wafers stacked vertically, optimizing space and facilitating continuous flow. Both types are essential for processes like doping and oxidation in semiconductor fabrication, impacting efficiency and yield in chip production.</p></p>
