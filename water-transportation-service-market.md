<p><ul><li>Maritime Transport</li><li>Fluvial Transport</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water transportation services encompass two main types: maritime transport and fluvial transport. Maritime transport involves the movement of goods and passengers over oceans and seas, utilizing large vessels such as cargo ships and tankers. This method is ideal for international trade due to its capacity and cost-effectiveness. Fluvial transport, on the other hand, refers to transportation along rivers and inland waterways, typically using smaller vessels like barges and ferries. It facilitates regional trade and connectivity while offering efficient alternatives to road transport.</p></p>
