<p><strong>The Acoustic Emission Based NDT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multiple Channel Source Location Techniques</li><li>Linear Location Techniques</li><li>Zonal Location Techniques</li><li>Point Location Techniques</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acoustic Emission Based NDT market encompasses various location techniques for identifying the source of acoustic emissions in materials. Multiple Channel Source Location Techniques utilize multiple sensors to triangulate sound, improving accuracy. Linear Location Techniques assess emissions along a defined line, suitable for pipelines. Zonal Location Techniques divide areas into zones for emission detection, enhancing fault identification. Point Location Techniques focus on isolating sound sources to a specific point, ideal for localized inspections. Each method offers unique advantages in non-destructive testing applications.</p></p>
