<p><p>The Active Optical Connector (AOC) market encompasses various types, each designed for specific applications in data transmission. QSFP (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable) supports high data rates, ideal for data centers. CXP offers similar performance but caters to 40G connectivity. CDFP serves a 100G Ethernet application, while CFP focuses on high-speed interfaces like 100G and beyond. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) is widely used for lower-speed connections. Other connectors include those tailored for niche applications or emerging technologies, expanding market diversity.</p></p>
