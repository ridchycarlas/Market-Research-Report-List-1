<p><p>Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) Units are advanced ventilation systems designed to improve indoor air quality while optimizing energy efficiency. These units work by extracting stale air from inside a building and replacing it with fresh, filtered outdoor air. The key feature is their ability to recover heat from the outgoing air and transfer it to the incoming air, significantly reducing heating costs and energy consumption.</p><p>The MVHR units market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing awareness of energy-efficient solutions and rising demand for sustainable building practices.
