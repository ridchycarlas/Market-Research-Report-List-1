<p><ul><li>Side Heating Type</li><li>Direct Heating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot-wire anemometers are precision instruments used to measure airflow velocity based on the cooling effect of air on a heated wire. The market is primarily categorized into two types: side heating and direct heating. Side heating types involve a wire heated on the side, offering enhanced sensitivity and response time, ideal for low-speed measurements. In contrast, direct heating types heat the wire directly in the airflow, providing robust measurements across a broader range of speeds, making them versatile for various applications.</p></p>
