<p><p>The live vaccines market is categorized into monovalent and multivalent live vaccines. Monovalent live vaccines contain a single strain of a pathogen and target a specific disease, providing focused protection. In contrast, multivalent live vaccines comprise multiple strains or types, offering broader immunity against several diseases within one formulation. Both types play crucial roles in public health by preventing infectious diseases and controlling outbreaks, but they differ in complexity and the range of protection they provide.</p></p>
