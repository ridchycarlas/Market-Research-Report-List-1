<p><p>Document Management Systems (DMS) can be categorized into three market types: on-premise, cloud-based, and hybrid. On-premise DMS is installed locally on company servers, offering high security and control. Cloud-based DMS is hosted on remote servers, providing accessibility and scalability with lower upfront costs. Hybrid DMS combines elements of both, allowing organizations to store sensitive documents on-premise while utilizing cloud solutions for less critical data, thus balancing security and flexibility according to specific needs.</p></p>
