<p><p>The DLP projection lens market consists of two primary types: fixed focus lenses and variable focal length lenses. Fixed focus lenses are designed for specific projection distances, providing consistent image quality but limited flexibility. In contrast, variable focal length lenses offer adjustable zoom capabilities, allowing for diverse projection distances and image sizes. This adaptability makes them suitable for various applications, including home theaters and professional presentations, catering to users who require versatility in their projection setups.</p></p>
